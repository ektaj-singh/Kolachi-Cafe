About Us

Welcome to Kolachi Cafe and Eatery, a stylish gem in the heart of Napier, the vibrant Art Deco city. Recently renovated, our cafe seamlessly blends history and modernity, offering breakfast and lunch delights seven days a week.

At Kolachi, we believe that breakfast and lunch are not just meals but opportunities to create cherished memories. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted with the tantalizing aromas of freshly brewed coffee and the promise of delicious dishes crafted with love and care. Our diverse menu caters to all tastes, from hearty classics to innovative and wholesome options, served seven days a week.

Whether you're seeking a delightful start to your day or a relaxing lunch with friends and family, Kolachi Cafe and Eatery welcomes you with open arms. Join us as we celebrate the rich flavors of Napier, where culinary excellence meets a vibrant cultural experience. Step into Kolachi, and let us take you on a gastronomic journey like no other.
